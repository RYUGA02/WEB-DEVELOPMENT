My Top 3 Movies Of All Time.

Forrest Gump

"Forrest Gump" is a 1994 American drama film directed by Francis Lawrence Fisher. The film is based on the bestselling novel "Forrest Gump" by Winston Groom. The movie stars Tom Hanks as Forrest Gump, a dim-witted yet physically fit and strong man who enlists in the United States Army during the Vietnam War. Throughout his life, he has many adventures and meets many notable figures, including life-long friend Bubba Gump and historic figures such as President John F. Kennedy and ping-pong champion Charlie Bird. The film is about his life, love, and the lessons he learns along the way.
